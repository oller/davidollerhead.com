Hi there, welcome to my online portfolio.

As you may have gathered, my name is David Ollerhead and I'm a web designer. To aid with job applications, and a bit of shameless self-promotion, I've created this little website.

I have developed a keen interest in web design since our first family PC came with a modem (A PC which I fear I monopolised). Starting with applications such as HotDog and Paint Shop Pro I started making my little contribution to the world wide web from the spare bedroom. Fast forward a few years, and after A Levels, I went on to study Computer Science + E-Business at Loughborough University (Currently 6th in The Times Good Univeristy Guide). A course and institution which I enjoyed immensely. After graduating and stepping out of the microcosm of student life, I became a full time web designer. Specialising in working within the Marketing Department to create e-Marketing campaigns and e-Communications.

hands off the family PC!

Since those early days in the spare room, the tools I use have evolved, Paint Shop Pro was inevitably superceded by the superb Adobe Photoshop, and now using Dreamweaver and Textpad to code. Despite these changes, the passion to create clear, well designed, intuitive and standard-compliant websites is stronger than ever. This passion often encroaches on my own time, I'm fascinated with learning new tricks and techniques. And photoshopping friends to look like criminals is as good a motivation as any other!

Despite this nerdy spiel, I'd like to stress I do like to have fun too! From looking around some of my University lecture theatres, it would be fair to say the stereotype of the average Computer Science student isn't far off at all. But I'd like to emphasise the 2 aren't mutually exclusive. I enjoy an active social life, and am in constant pursuit of getting the best out of London to consciously justify the rent!

So, please enjoy your perusal around my latest contribution to the wonderful internet and feel free to drop me a mail.

Thanks,

David
