Skirting and Architrave

At F A North (Carlton) Ltd we can machine to order any mould to match your exisiting in any timber. We have a huge selection of moulds and cutters. If, however, your mould is not avaialable, we can produce the cutters to match. We can run any quantity of moulded timber, but have a minimum length requirement of 3 metres.

Fax or e-mail your section and we will price by return.
