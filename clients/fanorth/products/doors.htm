Doors

F A North (Carlton) Ltd offer both Standard and Purpose Made doors, with all Purpose Made doors manufactured on Site in a variety of Hardwoods, preservative treated and either stained or primed and undercoated.

We offer a standard range of doors by J B Kind, including the Royal range of doors (see link), Jeldwen range of doors (see link), STP range of doors (see link) and Johnson Brother doors (catalogue only).

Purpose Made French Door sets are manufactured in Hardwood and can be supplied only or supplied and fitted. They come complete with either a five lever lock pack, bolts and handles or as an eight point locking system for added security. The doors can be supplied as singles or doubles, with side lights, fully or part glazed, or Georgian style with Planted Bar design.

Folding Door Sets

Up to seven doors fold aside using a Henderson Hardware Gear. All manufactured in Hardwood for stability.

Entrance and Internal Doors

We can replicate any design, style or size. Manufactured in Hardwood or a high quality Softwood such as Douglas Fir. Single or Double Glazed complete with frame, again to any size or section. The Bespoke option offers you the possibility of having the exact product you require.

Fitting service available on all the above.

We can manufacture both internal and external doors in any style or design whether it be French doors, sliding and folding doors or straight forward front doors.  We also carry a large number of standard doors in stock from suppliers like J.B.Kind, Jeldwin and STP; a full fitting service is available.
