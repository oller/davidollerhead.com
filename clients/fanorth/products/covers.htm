Radiator Covers

Our Radiator covers are supplied in a choice of either - finished White, Primed, or Unfinished.

They are fully assembled and with a choice of M.D.F. or Metal Grills.

We have a choice of two main styles, either, a fixed box type. Or one which allows you to open up side covers at each end of the Radiator cover, to access valves on Radiator, and without moving the cover from the wall.

The maximum width of cover is 3050mm.

Although we have two general styles, these can be adapted to suit your requirements and can be produced in veneered or solid hardwoods.
