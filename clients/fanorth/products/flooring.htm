Flooring

We can supply or supply and fit decorative timber flooring from most of the leading Manufacturers, from a straight forward 7mm Laminate Click flooring to 28mm wide board structured engineered Oak. We have it all.

In solid and engineered flooring, we are able to offer flooring in 16 species, ranging from the ever popular American White Oak to the more obscure such as Rokfah (Asian Walnut), Wenge and Jatoba. Finishes available are Oiled, Waxed or Unfinished.

The products available are Laminate and Engineered (these come in 10mm, 15mm, 18mm and 28mm).

We also offer Parquet blocks.
