Windows

F A North (Carlton) Ltd manufacture windows to match existing designs, but using modern techniques and products to replicate the traditional. We manufacture in either Hardwood or Softwood, or a combination of both. All frames are vac vac treated, primed and undercoated or stained with two coats of microporous stain. All windows are manufactured to comply with FENSA regulations and requirements and incorporate weather seals to openers, trickle head ventilation and Pilkington K glass double glazed units. We also provide a choice of furniture in both design and finish. We also offer Multi-Point locking systems for extra security.

We can manufacture Traditional Casements Windows, either as Storm-proof Casements, Flush Casements, and as Bay windows, square or splayed and Arch Top windows and either Full Pane or Multi-Pane with our T6 Planted Bar System.

We also manufacture York Sliders (Horizontal Sliding Windows) in Full Pane or Multi-Pane, Dummy Sash or True Sash. (Please see our Sash page for further details).

Fitting service is available on all the above.
