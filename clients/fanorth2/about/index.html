F.A. North (Carlton) Limited commenced Trading in 1930 and produced most joinery items.  The current Directors are now into their Thirty third year and the company has expanded considerably.  The type of joinery products manufactured on site, including Window frames, Doors, Specialist Joinery, including items required in Listed Buildings i.e. Sliding Sashes, all kinds Bay Bow and Georgian Window Frames.  We are also able to supply and fit double Glazed Units.  Window Frames will comply with current Building Regulations.  We have been Members of the British Woodworking Federation for many years.
