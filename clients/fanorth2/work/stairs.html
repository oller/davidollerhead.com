We can supply stairs in a variety of materials - anything from basic white wood with MDF treads through to Oak and American Black Walnut. We can offer a full design service with site visits and measuring available. We can provide Traditional designs with a spindle matching service. All our stairs are custom made, so many features can be incorporated, for example, volutes, bull nose tread, continuous handrails, cut string and open riser are all available.

We also produce treads and risers to suit steel and glass stairs, all machined to your specifications.

We can produce stairs in the following materials, or a combination:

  • Ash
  • Beech
  • American White Oak
  • European Oak
  • Poplar
  • Parana Pine
  • Maple
  • Hemlock
  • Red wood
  • West African Mahogany
  • Sapelle
  • Idigbo
  • White-wood
  • American Black Walnut
  • MDF

We also offer a full fitting service within our local area.
